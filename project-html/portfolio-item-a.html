welcome to China!

Here I will briefly introduce what I know, what I saw in China, and what I learned.

I start from being interested in everyone! First of all, I was born in a place where all the Kazakhs were born (of course there will still be Chinese people, after all, it is their territory!). We don't eat insects! We don't eat! Okay, next one! The life of our family is very traditional. More traditional than the people of Kazakhstan!
If you let me talk about learning, many teachers are Chinese and very strict! Class starts early in the morning, seven o'clock! There is a two-hour break at noon! We have to go to class until five in the afternoon!
In terms of language, the school has two kinds of education, one is specially prepared for the minority people, and the other is for the Chinese. The first type of education has its own language and Chinese, and the second type of education has Chinese and English! I chose the first one!
