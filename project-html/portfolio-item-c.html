Welcome to Kazakhstan!

I'm back to the land of my own blood! Very happy!

I came to Kazakhstan in the winter of 2016. I have graduated in seventh grade in China! But the reason for the difference in the tutorials is that I have to go to the seventh grade again! To be honest, what I thought before coming to Kazakhstan was that I could return to my own country! I can speak the Kazakh language anytime I am very excited! Unexpectedly, after I came, I discovered that everyone speaks Russian. It was really annoying and disgusting! Later, I slowly accepted the reality! I still remember when the national flag was raised for the first time, when I sang: "менің елім", my tears flowed out unknowingly!
